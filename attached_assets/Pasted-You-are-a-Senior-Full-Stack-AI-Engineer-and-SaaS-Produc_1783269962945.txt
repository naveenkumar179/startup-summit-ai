=========================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
=========================================================

StartupBridge AI is an AI-powered platform connecting Startup Founders with Investors.

There are 3 user roles:

1. Founder
2. Investor
3. Admin

Founders upload startup information and a Pitch Deck (PDF).

Investors discover startups and use AI to analyze them before contacting founders.

AI is the core feature of this application.

This should look like a modern SaaS product similar to Linear, Notion, Stripe Dashboard, Vercel Dashboard and PitchBook.
